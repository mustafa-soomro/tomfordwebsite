Tom Ford - Fashion and Fragrance Designer





Description

Thomas Carlyle Ford is an American fashion designer and filmmaker. Ford acted in television commercials before moving onto Parsons, where he studied architecture. After quitting, he was hired by other luxury brands.
Accomplishments

He launched his eponymous luxury brand in 2005, having previously served as the creative director at Gucci and Yves Saint Laurent. Ford wrote and directed the films A Single Man and Nocturnal Animals.
Luxury Brand Tom Ford

After leaving Gucci, Ford launched a line of menswear, beauty, eyewear, and accessories in 2006, named after himself, Tom Ford. This brand evolved to create fragrances, accessories, and much more.
